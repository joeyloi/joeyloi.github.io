Humans of Shopify

I feel that the best kept secret about Shopify is the diversity of the people who work here. A small team of us work to share our stories to show the outside world just how vibrant and rich our culture is.


Classtronaut

Coming out of high school, I was frustrated with how disconnected my education felt from the real world. My most valuable learning came from things I worked on outside of school, and so I wanted to bring real world projects into high school classrooms to replicate a similar experience for other students. I partnered with an NGO and two world issues classes and ran a pilot for a project where students helped the NGO develop resources to educate youth in developing communities about health and social issues. I stopped working on Classtronaut after realizing fundamental flaws in the model and a shift in priorities that came with school.
